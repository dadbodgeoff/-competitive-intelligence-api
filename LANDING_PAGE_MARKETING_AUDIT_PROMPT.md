## üí° SPECIAL INSTRUCTIONS

### For AI/LLM Features:
- Explain what the AI does in plain English
- Avoid technical jargon like "embeddings" or "vector search"
- Focus on user benefit: "Automatically extracts..." not "Uses GPT-4 to..."

### For Technical Features:
- Translate to business value
- "Real-time streaming" ‚Üí "See results as they happen"
- "Fuzzy matching" ‚Üí "Automatically links similar items"
- "SSE" ‚Üí "Live updates without refreshing"

### For Metrics:
- Extract ANY numbers from code, comments, or docs
- Processing times, accuracy rates, data volumes, etc.
- Even estimates are valuable for marketing

### For Proof Points:
- Look for complexity indicators (# of steps, # of data points)
- Look for sophistication indicators (AI, real-time, automation)
- Look for scale indicators (handles X items, processes Y documents)
